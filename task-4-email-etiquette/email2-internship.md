## 📝 Why This Email Follows Professional Rules

| Element | How It Is Used in This Email |
|---------|------------------------------|

| Greeting | Formal — "Dear Sir/Madam" (when name is unknown) |
| Introduction | Name, reg no., institution, and course clearly stated |

| Body Para 2 | Highlights skills, platforms, and motivation |

| Sign-off | Professional with GitHub and LinkedIn links included |
