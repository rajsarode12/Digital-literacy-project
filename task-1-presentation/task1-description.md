## 🛠️ Tool Used: Canva

**What is Canva?**
Canva is a free, browser-based graphic design tool that allows
anyone — even without design experience — to create beautiful
posters, infographics, presentations, and more. It offers
thousands of ready-made templates that can be customized easily.

**Why I Chose Canva:**
- Completely free to use
- No installation required (works in browser)
- Has ready-made infographic templates
- Easy drag-and-drop interface
- Perfect for first-time designers

**Canva Website:** https://www.canva.com

## 📋 Step-by-Step: How I Created the Infographic

### Step 1 — Sign Up on Canva
1. Go to https://www.canva.com
2. Click "Sign Up" and register using your college email address
3. Select "Student" as your purpose

### Step 2 — Choose a Template
1. In the search bar, type **"Digital Literacy Infographic"**
   or **"Education Infographic"**
2. Browse the free templates (look for ones marked FREE)
3. Click on a template you like to open it in the editor

### Step 3 — Customize the Infographic
Edit the template to include at least 3 of these topics:

**Topic 1 — What is Digital Literacy?**
> Digital Literacy is the ability to use digital devices,
> communication tools, and internet safely, responsibly,
> and effectively to find, evaluate, create, and share
> information.

**Topic 2 — Useful Digital Tools for Students**
> - GitHub – Store and share code projects
> - Google Workspace – Docs, Sheets, Forms for collaboration
> - Canva – Design presentations and infographics
> - HackerRank – Practice coding and earn certificates
> - LinkedIn – Build professional network

**Topic 3 — Safe Internet Practices**
> - Use strong, unique passwords for every account
> - Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
> - Never click on unknown or suspicious links
> - Do not share personal information online
> - Always log out from shared devices

**Topic 4 — Professional Online Presence*
> - Keep your GitHub and LinkedIn profiles updated
> - Use a professional profile picture
> - Post only content you would show to an employer
> - Engage positively with your professional community

**Topic 5 — Email Etiquette** 
> - Always use a clear subject line
> - Address the recipient professionally
> - Keep your message concise and respectful
> - Always proofread before sending

### Step 4 — Design Tips
- Use **2-3 colors** only (keep it clean and readable)
- Use **icons** to represent each topic visually
- Keep **font size** large enough to read easily
- Add your **name and reg number** at the bottom
- Use a **light background** with dark text for readability

### Step 5 — Export the Infographic
1. Click the **"Share"** button (top right corner in Canva)
2. Select **"Download"**
3. Choose format: **PNG** or **JPG**
4. Click **"Download"** to save the file to your device


## 🔗 How to Get Your Canva Shareable Link

1. Open your design in Canva
2. Click **"Share"** (top right)
3. Click **"Share a link"**
4. Set it to **"Anyone with the link can view"**
5. Copy the link and paste it in `canva-link.md` file
