### Platform 3 — Kaggle

**What is Kaggle?**
Kaggle is an online platform owned by Google, specifically
designed for Data Science, Machine Learning, and Artificial
Intelligence. It provides free datasets, interactive coding
notebooks, beginner-to-advanced courses, and real-world
competitions where participants solve AI/ML problems. Companies
actively look at Kaggle rankings and medals when hiring data
scientists and AI engineers.

**What I Did on Kaggle:**
