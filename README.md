## 🎯 Project Overview

This repository is my **Digital Literacy Portfolio**, created as
part of the CSE0001 – Digital Literacy course at VIT Bhopal
University. The project is based on the scenario where I am
working as a **Student Digital Ambassador** — helping my peers
navigate the digital world safely, professionally, and
responsibly.

The portfolio is organized into **five tasks**, each mapping to
one module of the course. Together, they cover:
- Creating visual digital content (Infographic)
- Building a professional online presence (Digital Portfolio)
- Exploring coding and collaboration platforms
- Professional email writing and social media etiquette
- Cybercrime awareness and prevention

All tools used in this project are **free and browser-based**.
No prior coding knowledge was required.

### ✅ Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention Guide

**What I Did:**
Researched and wrote a detailed case study on UPI/Online Payment
Fraud — the most common cybercrime targeting college students in
India — and created a practical prevention checklist.

**Part A – Case Study:**
- **Topic:** UPI / Online Payment Fraud
- **Story:** The Fake Scholarship Fraud — How a first-year
  student lost ₹15,000 through a fake UPI collect request
- **Covers:** What the crime is → How it happens (5 steps) →
  Who is targeted → Consequences

**Part B – Prevention Checklist:**
Created a "Stay Safe Online" checklist with **12 actionable tips**
for college students in India.

**Key Prevention Tips:**
- 🔐 Never enter your UPI PIN to RECEIVE money
- 🔐 Always check if a UPI request is a collect request
- 🔐 Never share OTP, CVV, or banking passwords with anyone
- 🔐 Enable 2FA on all important accounts
- 🔐 Verify before clicking any link from unknown sources

**🚨 Report Cybercrime:**
- 🌐 Online: **cybercrime.gov.in**
- 📞 Helpline: **1930** (24x7, Free, Pan-India)

**Files in task-5-cybercrime/:**
